Our Story

It started with two students and a professor who believed in me.

During college, my professor asked me to tutor two struggling students and help mentor them through to graduation. I said yes. What happened next changed everything — I watched two people who were on the verge of giving up rediscover their confidence, push through, and graduate.

That's when I realized: there are thousands of students out there who don't need a miracle. They just need someone in their corner.

NextGen Tutoring exists for those students — the ones who are one good session away from getting it, one caring mentor away from believing in themselves, one breakthrough away from everything changing.

Hafeez Oyeneyin
Founder, NextGen Tutoring
