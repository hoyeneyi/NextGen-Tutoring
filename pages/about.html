Who I Am

My name is Hafeez — founder of NextGen Tutoring, educator, and lifelong learner. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Engineering and a Master’s in Artificial Intelligence, but my passion lies in helping students truly understand what they’re learning and gain confidence in their abilities.

I've worked with a wide range of students — from 3rd graders learning multiplication to college students tackling calculus and logic. I specialize in math, but I’m equally comfortable guiding students through science, grammar, and standardized test prep.

What sets my sessions apart is that I treat tutoring as more than just academic help. I focus on building study habits, critical thinking, and real self-belief. I tailor every session based on the student’s needs — breaking down complex topics into simple, engaging lessons that stick.
