The Story

It started with two students
and a belief in second chances.

During college, my professor pulled me aside and asked me to tutor two struggling high school students — to sit with them, mentor them, and help them find their way. I said yes without hesitation.

What happened over the next few months changed my life. I watched two young people who were on the verge of giving up rediscover their confidence, push through every obstacle, and get back on track.

That's when I realized something I can't un-see: the difference between a student who makes it and one who doesn't often comes down to one thing — someone who believes in them. Not a system. Not a program. A person.

NextGen Tutoring exists to be that person for every student who needs it.

Hafeez Oyeneyin
Founder, NextGen Tutoring
Our Mission

In too many communities, the students who need the most support get the least of it.

"We see it every day in Metro Detroit. Brilliant kids falling behind not because they aren't capable — but because nobody sat with them, explained it differently, and told them they could do it."

NextGen Tutoring was built for those kids. We are from this community. We understand this community. And we are committed to building something that serves this community for generations.

Today that looks like personalized tutoring sessions. Tomorrow it looks like learning centers, free digital resources, and programs that reach every student who needs them — regardless of what they can afford.

This is bigger than tutoring. This is about what our community's next generation becomes.
