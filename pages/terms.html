Code of Conduct

NextGen Tutoring is committed to providing a safe, respectful, and productive learning environment for every student. We expect the following from all parties:

Students and parents/guardians agree to:

  • Treat the tutor with respect in all communications and sessions
  • Arrive on time and prepared for each session
  • Provide a safe and distraction-appropriate environment for virtual or in-home sessions
  • Not record sessions without the express written consent of NextGen Tutoring
  • Not share session materials, worksheets, or content created by NextGen Tutoring without permission

NextGen Tutoring agrees to:

  • Arrive on time and prepared for each session
  • Maintain the confidentiality of student information
  • Provide honest progress feedback to students and parents
  • Treat every student with patience, respect, and encouragement

NextGen Tutoring reserves the right to terminate the tutoring relationship immediately and without refund in cases of harassment, abuse, or repeated violation of this code of conduct.
